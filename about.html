Trainers of Car Repairs

Ashen Wishwageeth

Automotive technology instructors are the backbone of car repair education. They are experienced professionals with in-depth knowledge of automotive systems, including engines, transmissions, electrical systems, and more. These instructors work in technical schools, community colleges, and vocational training centers. They teach students the fundamentals of automotive repair, including diagnostics, maintenance, and repair.

Nuwan Drashna

Many car manufacturers have training programs and facilities where they employ specialized trainers. These trainers are experts in the specific brand they represent. They teach dealership technicians and service personnel about the latest models, advanced diagnostics, and repair techniques unique to that brand. Manufacturer-sponsored trainers ensure that the technicians at dealerships are well-equipped to handle repairs on their vehicles to manufacturer standards.

Gathika Colambage

ASE is an independent organization that certifies automotive technicians. Some experienced ASE-certified technicians become instructors in automotive repair programs. These instructors have proven their expertise by passing rigorous ASE exams. They teach students not only how to pass these exams but also practical, real-world automotive repair skills. Their focus is on preparing students for ASE certification, which is highly regarded

Chamuthu Pabasara

In the digital age, there is a growing community of online educators and YouTube creators who offer tutorials and educational content related to car repairs. These individuals often have extensive experience in automotive repair and share their knowledge through video tutorials, blogs, and social media. While they may not offer formal certification, they provide valuable supplementary education for enthusiasts and DIY mechanic.
